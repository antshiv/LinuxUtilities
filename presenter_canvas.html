Presenter Canvas

Live whiteboard for recording. Draw, drop icons, animate flow lines, and save/load JSON.

Artboards

Layers

Artboard is the base layer. Shape layers are ordered top to bottom. Click a grouped shape to select its group; Ctrl+click selects only that layer.

Shapes

Quick insert at viewport center using current style settings.

Script Editor

One Block: whole script → one clip on the timeline. Words timed by WPM, displayed N-at-a-time. Drag the clip to reposition; click words in the list to seek.

Bottom timeline editor: drag clips to move, drag edges to trim, Shift+Click multi-select, X split at playhead.

Tools

Quick keys: 1..9 tools, Shift snap angle/ratio, Del delete, Ctrl+Z/Y, F focus, H hide/show panel. With Snap On, drag snaps to grid and nearby shape centers/edges.
